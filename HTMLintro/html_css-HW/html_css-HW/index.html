Here's my Review of Din Tai Fung

My personal rating on Din Tai Fung is a 10/10. When recommending a place to try in bellevue I would definetly suggest this one !!
To start, the service is always very good, getting your order in is smooth and the food comes out pretty fast. Now the food, which is the most important, obviously. I love getting a variety of things like the fried rice, xai long bao, and the pepper steak. Everytime it comes out tasting amazing, fresh, and flavorful. Also, if your a fan of boba their milk tea is also great! Lastly, the best thing they have on their menu, the chocolate xai long bao!! This dessert is one of the best desserts out there, it comes with 6 mini buns in an order. These taste like mini chocolate crepes, and the gooey chocolate comes out very warm, so this is something I would never skip if I'm stopping by. Overall, this resturaunt is a must try if you haven't.
